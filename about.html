About Us

Our Story

The company was established by Engr. Rizal Alberto Nolido on May 1969 in Manila as R.A. NOLIDO & COMPANY, INC. primarily to complement the nationwide demand for housing construction and subdivision development. It then ventured into warehouse and superstructure building and engineering works, reaching its heights during the construction peak of the seventies into the eighties.


In 1991, it was re-incorporated and renamed R.A. NOLIDO CONSTRUCTION CORPORATION (RANCC), with Engr. Ramon Alberto Nolido as President. It has had projects from institutional, industrial, commercial, condominiums and on occasion residential.


Through time, the company increased its Management and Engineering Staff and has been conducting technical support and skills training seminars for its staff on a regular basis. It also maintains a yard in Santa Maria Bulacan.


The company has since evolved and provides construction management service for different projects but mostly for colleges and universities. Through time, the company has. Having been a professor at UP Diliman, Engr. Nolido and his team also engages in engineering design for structural, electrical, plumbing, fire protection and mechanical works as well as architecture.


With integrity as its primary currency, the company is ready more than ever to face the challenges of a demanding industry.
